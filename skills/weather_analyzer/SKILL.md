# Weather Analyzer

## Instructions

- Read the provided weather conditions carefully.
- Identify the following:
  - Temperature level (low, moderate, high)
  - Humidity level (low, medium, high)
  - Rain probability (none, low, high)
  - Wind condition (calm or strong)

- Summarize the overall weather condition in simple terms.
- Highlight any potentially risky weather patterns.
