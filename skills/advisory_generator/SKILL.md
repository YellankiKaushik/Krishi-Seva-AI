# Advisory Generator

## Instructions

- Convert the identified risks into clear, actionable advice.
- Use simple, farmer-friendly language.
- Keep sentences short and direct.
- Focus only on what the farmer should do.

- Provide:
  - What to do
  - What to avoid

## Output Format

Advice:
Do:
Avoid:
